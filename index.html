Practical Python for Chandra Engineers¶

Practical Python for Chandra Engineers is a series of hands-on workshops and examples used to demonstrate and explore the capability of Python language. We will primarily use material already available on the internet with a few special topic sessions.

This documentation and these workshops are inspired by the material produced by Tom Aldcroft et al. for the Python4Astronomers seminar given during the spring of 2011.
