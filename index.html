Practical Python for Chandra Engineers¶

Practical Python for Chandra Engineers is a series of hands-on workshops and examples used to demonstrate and explore the capability of Python language. This documentation and these workshops are inspired by the material produced by Tom Aldcroft et al. for the Python4Astronomers seminar given during the spring of 2011.
